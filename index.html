The E-Equation

A heuristic for measuring contribution to the field

E = (N × S) / C

N — Connection

The network density. Nodes strengthened, relationships maintained. Without connection, intelligence becomes solipsistic.

S — Signal

The truth value. Coherence, durability, generativity. Without signal, connection is merely noise.

C — Cost

The thermodynamic tax. Energy expended, friction generated. Entropy is the filter through which all action passes.
