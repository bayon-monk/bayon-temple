The most expensive lesson in technology

AI has made every individual in your organisation more productive. But productive individuals do not make productive firms.

Most organisations are bolting AI capability onto existing structures. The technology is far superior. The organisation is not.

The result: AI pilots that stall at deployment. Agentic systems that create friction faster than value. Internal teams shipping software fast without considering the business model, the customer, or the governance. Executives pressing pause because transformation feels too risky while the current business needs to keep running.

The organisations that win will not be the first to adopt AI. They will be the first to redesign how they operate around it.
